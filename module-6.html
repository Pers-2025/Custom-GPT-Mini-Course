Module 6: Publish and Share

You've built your Custom GPT. Now it’s time to make it live and start sharing it with the world!

Step 1: Publish Your GPT

In the GPT Builder, go to the “Configure” tab and click Publish. You can choose to make it:

  • Public: Anyone can find and use it in the GPT store.
  • Private: Only those with your link can access it (ideal for client projects or internal tools).

Step 2: Write a Compelling Description

Your GPT description is your “sales pitch.” Briefly explain:

  • What it does
  • Who it helps
  • What makes it unique

Step 3: Share It!

Start spreading the word! Post the link on your:

  • Website or blog
  • Email newsletter
  • Social media
  • LinkedIn profile
  • WhatsApp groups or client messages

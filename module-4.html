Module 4: Build with GPT Builder

Now it’s time to build! You’ll use OpenAI’s built-in GPT Builder to bring your GPT to life.

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Log in to ChatGPT with your Plus account.
  2. Click the “Explore GPTs” button in the sidebar, then click “Create”.
  3. Choose between “Easy” (guided) or “Advanced” mode — you’ll be using “Advanced” in this course.
  4. Enter your GPT’s Name and a short Greeting Message.
  5. Paste your full Instructions in the system prompt. This is what shapes how your GPT behaves.
  6. Optional: Upload files or docs under the “Knowledge” section.
  7. Enable/disable features like code interpreter, DALL·E, or browsing — depending on your use case.
  8. Test your GPT with prompts and revise the instructions as needed.
  9. Once satisfied, click Publish. Set your visibility (public or private).

Custom GPT Creation Mini Course

Learn how to create, structure, and publish your very own Custom GPT — even if you're not a developer. In just one day, you’ll have your own assistant live, useful, and sharable.

Module 2: Prompting Logic

Module 3: File Uploads & Knowledge

Module 5: Testing & Use Cases

Module 6: Publishing & Sharing

Customize branding, publish privately or publicly, and generate your unique GPT link.

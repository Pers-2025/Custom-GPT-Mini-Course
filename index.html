Custom GPT Creation Mini Course

A step-by-step guide to help you create and publish your own Custom GPT — even if you're not technical. This 6-module course gives you everything you need to build your first GPT in a single day.

What You’ll Learn

Module 1: Overview & Setup

Learn how GPTs work, what's possible, and how to prepare your idea and structure.

Module 2: Prompting Logic

Design your GPT’s behavior using clear instructions and strategic examples.

Module 3: Uploads & Knowledge

Teach your GPT using files, FAQs, product guides, or internal docs.

Module 4: Actions (APIs)

Extend your GPT with real-time features like calculators or databases using APIs.

Module 5: Testing & Use Cases

Refine your GPT and explore business or personal applications.

Module 6: Publishing & Sharing

Customize branding, privacy, access, and launch your GPT to the world.

Module 1: Introduction

Welcome! In this module, you'll learn what Custom GPTs are and why they matter. You don’t need coding skills — just a goal, a problem to solve, and some strategic thinking.

Custom GPTs allow you to create AI assistants that speak your language, follow your tone, and help you with repetitive work like content creation, research, lesson planning, or client onboarding.

Reflection Activity: Why do you want to create a Custom GPT? What problem or task do you hope it will help you with?
